Who We Are

Rongali is a human rights organization for individuals oppressed due to their sexaul orenitatation and a refuge for sex trafficking vitcims across India. We provide shelter, education, food, medical, and many other services to women, men, children, and LGBTQ individuals. Vulnerable populations are frequently targeted by traffickers, including runaway and homeless youth, as well as victims of domestic violence, sexual assault, war, or social discrimination. Sexuality minorities include, but are not limited to, women, lesbians, bisexuals, homosexuals, Female-to-male/male-to-female transsexuals, and other transgender. we aim to help live their lives with self-acceptance, self-respect and dignity. Rangoli aims to bring sexuality, sexual preference and gender identity into the realm of public discourse and link it to gender, human rights development and other social movements.
